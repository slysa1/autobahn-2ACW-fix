Critical Decision Rules
Patch a mod if:
- the mod is valuable to the user,
- the break is clearly localized,
- and the required fix surface is small.

Remove or disable a mod if:
- it wins broad foundational files,
- it generates large repeat-spam against dead ideas/techs,
- or it requires large stale forks just to load cleanly.
